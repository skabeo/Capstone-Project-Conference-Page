ENTERTAINMENT
2023

Thank you for joining us in celebrating the vibrant and diverse cultures of Africa at Afrochella. Stay connected with us to continue the journey of empowering, educating, and entertaining the world about the beauty of Africa.

2023.12.23(SAT) ~ 27(WED)

@ El wak sports stadium Accra and National Theatre

Main Program

Bus Trip

A bus trip organized by Afronation is a fun-filled and vibrant experience, featuring lively music and enthusiastic passengers.

Lecture

Join us for an enlightening lecture session at Afrochella, where experts in their fields will delve into the rich cultural heritage.

Presents

Immerse yourself in the vibrant culture of Africa at Afrochella's Present section, where you can acquire unique traditional artifacts.

Forum

Engage in thought-provoking discussions and exchange ideas with like-minded individuals at Afrochella's Forum section.

Exhibition

Discover diversity where talented artists will showcase their works inspired by the rich cultural heritage of the continent.
